Korea: You Are Not Alone Online Guidebook for Living in Korea

In Progress

Living in a foreign country is a wonderful experience that helps people learn new things and grow fast. However, there are times when foreigners feel frustrated because they cannot figure out how to do even a simple task. On such occasions, having a friend or someone who can help would be great, but finding such a person is not easy and asking every little question may be hard as it seems like a bother.

Fortunately, we are living in an age where people can be connected to each other or gather information, much more conveniently. As an example, there are many Instagrammers in Korea, creating useful content about living in this country and preparing guidelines even about doing those simple tasks.

However, the problem is that it is not always easy to find the right content at the right time. Plus, asking a question and receiving an answer on the right time may not be possible. Therefore, a dedicated platform would be a great help.

Korea: You Are Not Alone would be an application where experienced foreigners in Korea or native Koreans who are interested in helping newcomers can post their informative content in a better organized format, so that finding information about a specific subject can be easily done with search and filters. Also, anyone can ask any question at any time on this platform, and anyone who knows can answer as soon as possible.

I am working on this project as UX Designer, and it is still in the first stages of research which is a very important step for the rest of the process.
