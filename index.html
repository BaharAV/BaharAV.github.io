I am a MS student in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Kyungpook National University in Daegu, South Korea (GPA 4.3/4.3). Currently, I am working on anomaly detection using zero/few-shot learning and multimodal models, at the Multimedia Information Processing laboratory while serving as a teaching assistant. My research interests include machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, multimodal (vision-language) systems, and various applications. I aim to deepen my understanding of the fundamental principles in these areas and to develop novel, efficient, and trustworthy solutions for various applications.
